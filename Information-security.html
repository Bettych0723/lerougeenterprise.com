Innovative Information Security Solutions for Modern Enterprises

At our company, we specialize in providing cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions tailored to the needs of software development and IT environments. Our suite of services empowers enterprises to protect their digital assets with advanced threat intelligence, network visibility, and proactive defense strategies. Here's how we safeguard your business:

LEROUGEInsight

Unlock the power of global and localized threat intelligence to strengthen your defenses. With a focus on Taiwan-specific intelligence and international exchange insights, LeRougeInsight enables enterprises to simulate and prepare for diverse cyber threats in their internal environments.

Key Features

Comprehensive Threat Intelligence

Harness both global and regional threat insights.

Smart Intelligence Categorization

Organize and prioritize threats for actionable prevention.

Proactive Attack Mitigation

Implement targeted measures to stop attacks before they occur.

LEROUGEViewer

Achieve unparalleled network visibility with LEROUGEViewer, designed to analyze netflow and syslog data from multiple devices. This solution precisely detects malicious behaviors, tracks suspicious activity, and helps IT teams ensure operational security with integrated tools.

Key Features

Accurate Attack Detection

Identify threats with precision.

Lateral Movement Visibility

Track the spread of attacks within your network.

Integrated Security Management

Seamlessly connect with various security tools for enhanced efficiency.

LEROUGESeeker

LEROUGESeeker provides Managed Detection and Response (MDR) services to monitor and protect your network and endpoints around the clock. By leveraging expert analysis and automated tools, this solution ensures early detection and elimination of risks, safeguarding your enterprise's critical assets.

Key Features

Expert Security Forensics

Regular reports and insights from cybersecurity specialists.

Automated Threat Detection

Identify malicious activities and vulnerabilities in real time.

Instant Alerts and Notifications

Immediate warnings of critical threats.

Root Cause Analysis

Visualize and trace attack origins and lateral movements.

LEROUGEDNS

Enhance your security at the DNS level with LEROUGEDNS. This solution blocks fraudulent websites, phishing attempts, and malicious links while providing detailed analytics, freeing IT teams to focus on strategic tasks.

Key Features

Malicious Website Blocking

Prevent access to harmful online resources.

Endpoint Anomaly Detection

Spot unusual domain connections.

Comprehensive DNS Traffic Reports

Gain actionable insights into DNS usage.
